The Weekly

A weekly operator newsletter on the ideas, company-building lessons, and market signals worth carrying into Monday.

About

The Weekly turns a week of podcasts and transcripts into a concise operator digest. It is written for founder-product and GTM people who care about revenue growth, market structure, distribution, capital allocation, and how high-agency companies actually run.

The aim is not to summarise everything. It is to find the useful signal: the contrarian read, the business implication, and the tactical move an operator can use.
