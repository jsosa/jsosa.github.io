About

This dashboard is a quick public-market screen across fintech, consumer internet, AI/data platforms, creative software, marketplaces and cloud infrastructure. It pulls the latest annual financials from Yahoo Finance via yfinance, then calculates YoY revenue growth, EBITDA margin, free-cash-flow margin, Rule of 40 and revenue multiple. Treat it as a first-pass comparison layer, not a full investment memo: yfinance can lag filings, and some international tickers may need manual checking.
